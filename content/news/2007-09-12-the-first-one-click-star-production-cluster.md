<div class="news-date">Sep 12, 2007</div>
<p>The <a href="https://www.star.bnl.gov/">STAR</a> community successfully completed its first production-size deployment of a VM-based virtual cluster managed by the workspace service and backed by EC2 resources.</p>
<p>The 100 node cluster was composed of a headnode and workernodes based on the <a href="https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/Documentation/WebHome">OSG 0.6.0</a> grid middleware stack and Torque. Its deployment-time configuration was securely coordinated by the new workspace contextualization technology.</p>
