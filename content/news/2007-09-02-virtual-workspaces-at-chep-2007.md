<div class="news-date">Sep 2-7, 2007</div>
<p>Our collaborators from <a href="https://www.uvic.ca/">University of Victoria</a> will present a talk at the <a href="https://www.chep2007.com/">CHEP 2007</a> conference summarizing their experiences using the Virtual Workspace Service to stand up and manage virtual machines.</p>
<p>The <a href="https://www.star.bnl.gov/">STAR</a> community also presented a talk describing the use of our <a href="https://workspace.globus.org/vm/deployments/index.html#teraport-ec2">EC2 portal</a> to run STAR applications. The slides are <a href="https://www.nimbusproject.org/news/downloads/OSGCHEP.pdf">available for download</a>.</p>
