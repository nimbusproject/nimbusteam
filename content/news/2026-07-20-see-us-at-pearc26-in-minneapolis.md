<p>The Nimbus team will be at <strong><a href="https://pearc.acm.org/pearc26/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">PEARC26</a></strong>, held July 26&ndash;30, 2026 at the Minneapolis Convention Center in Minneapolis, MN, under this year&rsquo;s theme &ldquo;Resilient Roots + Empowered Communities.&rdquo; PEARC brings together the advanced research computing community, and it is always one of our favorite chances to share our latest work and meet users of <a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Chameleon</a> and our other platforms face to face.</p>

<p>This year we are presenting <strong>&ldquo;Practical Evaluation Methods for Scientific Instruments&rdquo;</strong> (Kate Keahey, Paul Marshall, Mark Powers, Marc Richardson, Michael Sherman, and Dan Stanzione), which is available now via its DOI, <a href="https://doi.org/10.1145/3785462.3815804" target="_blank" rel="noopener">10.1145/3785462.3815804</a>, and as a <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org/media/filer_public/43/b3/43b348a7-c54e-4001-9216-634b8501e041/pearc26-15_4.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">PDF</a>. If you will be in Minneapolis, come say hello &mdash; and check back here afterward for a recap.</p>
