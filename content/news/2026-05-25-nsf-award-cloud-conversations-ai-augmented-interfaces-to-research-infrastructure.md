<p>We are excited to announce a new National Science Foundation award for <strong>&ldquo;Cloud Conversations: AI-Augmented Interfaces to Research Infrastructure,&rdquo;</strong> a collaborative project led by the Nimbus team at the University of Chicago. Cloud Conversations will build an AI-powered conversational interface that lets researchers describe what they need in plain language and have a suitable computing environment planned, provisioned, and validated for them across open research infrastructure &mdash; lowering the systems-expertise barrier that keeps many scientists from making full use of national testbeds and cloud platforms.</p>

<p>A central strength of the project is its partnership across three complementary platforms. The University of Chicago team (PI <a href="https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2609115" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kate Keahey</a>, with co-PI Kexin Pei) leads the framework and grounds it in <a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Chameleon</a>. <strong><a href="https://www.tacc.utexas.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">TACC</a></strong> (the Texas Advanced Computing Center, <a href="https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2609116" target="_blank" rel="noopener">award #2609116</a>, PI Niall Gaffney) and <strong><a href="https://jetstream-cloud.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Jetstream2</a></strong> at Indiana University (<a href="https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2609117" target="_blank" rel="noopener">award #2609117</a>, PI David Hancock) join as research-infrastructure partners, so that the natural-language interface is designed and validated against production platforms with very different architectures and user communities from the outset.</p>

<p>By targeting Chameleon, Jetstream2, and TACC together, Cloud Conversations aims to produce an interface that generalizes across the open research-infrastructure ecosystem rather than a single testbed. You can read more in the NSF award abstract for the lead award, <a href="https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2609115" target="_blank" rel="noopener">#2609115</a>. We look forward to sharing more as the project gets underway.</p>
