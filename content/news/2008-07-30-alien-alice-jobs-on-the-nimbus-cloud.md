<div class="news-date">Jul 30, 2008</div>
<p>In collaboration with Artem Harutyunyan and Predrag Buncic, <a href="https://alien.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/AliEn/Home">AliEn</a> based images are launching on Nimbus in support of the <a href="https://aliceinfo.cern.ch/Public/Welcome.html">ALICE</a> experiment to carry out simulation, reconstruction and distributed analysis of physics data. After one VM makes site services available, AliEn Job Agents can launch and retrieve jobs from the main task queue to execute.</p>
