<p>SC25 in St. Louis (November 16&ndash;21, 2025) was a busy week for our team. At the <strong>EduHPC 2025</strong> workshop, Kate Keahey presented <strong>&ldquo;The Cost of Teaching Operational ML&rdquo;</strong> (Fraida Fund, Kate Keahey, Cody Hammock, Marc Richardson, Mark Powers, and Michael Sherman), a <a href="https://fount.cs.uchicago.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">FOUNT</a> showcase that reports on running Fraida Fund&rsquo;s large-scale &ldquo;ML Systems Engineering and Operations&rdquo; course for ~191 students on Chameleon &mdash; nearly 187,000 compute-instance hours, at a fraction of the equivalent commercial-cloud cost. Read the <a href="https://fount.cs.uchicago.edu/2025/09/04/announcing-the-cost-of-teaching-operational-ml-a-fount-showcase-at-sc25-eduhpc/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">FOUNT announcement</a>, the full <a href="https://nimbusproject.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/116/2025/09/The_Cost_of_Teaching_Operational_ML_EduHPC.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">paper (PDF)</a> (DOI <a href="https://doi.org/10.1145/3731599.3767385" target="_blank" rel="noopener">10.1145/3731599.3767385</a>), or the <a href="https://fount.cs.uchicago.edu/2025/12/05/online-now-fraida-fund-nyu-presents-her-large-scale-mlops-on-chameleon/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">course materials and recording</a> now online through FOUNT.</p>

<p>Three of our students also brought their work to the <strong>ACM Student Research Competition</strong> poster session:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Hudson Reynolds and Alex Tuecke</strong> &mdash; an autonomous bioacoustic monitoring system for wildlife conservation, built on <a href="https://www.chameleoncloud.org/experiment/chiedge/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">CHI@Edge</a>.</li>
<li><strong>Zahra Temori</strong> &mdash; performance optimizations to the CC-snapshot tool, cutting snapshot creation time by roughly 80%.</li>
<li><strong>Saieda Ali Zada</strong> &mdash; an AI-powered documentation assistant that uses retrieval-augmented generation to help users navigate Chameleon resources.</li>
</ul>

<p>You can read the full recap, including the student spotlights, on the <a href="https://blog.chameleoncloud.org/posts/chameleon-at-sc25" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Chameleon at SC25 blog post</a>. Congratulations to everyone who presented!</p>
