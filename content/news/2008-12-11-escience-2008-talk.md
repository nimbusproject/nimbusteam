<div class="news-date">Dec 11, 2008</div>
<p>How to make a virtual cluster with one click: Kate will <a href="https://escience2008.iu.edu/sessions/contextualization.shtml">talk about our work on automated virtual cluster deployment</a> at the eScience conference on Thursday, December 11th at 1:30 pm.</p>
<p>Our virtual cluster deployment tools have been used to create production virtual clusters for scientific applications on EC2 as well as the Science Clouds for the past year.</p>
<p>And speaking of Science Clouds and virtual clusters: Mauricio Tsugawa will <a href="https://escience2008.iu.edu/sessions/cloudBlast.shtml">talk about CloudBLAST</a> and Ewa Deelman will <a href="https://escience2008.iu.edu/workshops/challenging/cloud.shtml">talk about Montage</a> virtual clusters.</p>
