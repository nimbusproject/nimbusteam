<p>Our paper about virtual appliance configuration and management was accepted to the TeraGrid 2007 conference and is now online: <i><a href="https://nimbusproject.org/files/Scalable_Approach_To_Deploying_And_Managing_Appliances.pdf">A Scalable Approach To Deploying And Managing Appliances</a></i></p>
<p>This paper examines configuration and security issues that large and heterogeneous deployments of virtual appliances/workspaces will face.</p>
