<p>Our short paper on enabling cost-effective resource leases was accepted to the Hot Topics session in the HPDC 2007 conference and is now online: <i><a href="https://nimbusproject.org/files/HPDC2007_hottopic_SotomayorKeaheyFosterFreeman.pdf">Enabling Cost-Effective Resource Leases with Virtual Machines</a></i></p>
<p>This paper discusses how virtualization can facilitate short-term leasing of resources, while allowing resource providers to continue support for existing batch workloads and their current job execution software stack. The paper discusses preliminary results obtained so far, and future work in our group on an architecture to support cost-effective resource leasing.</p>
