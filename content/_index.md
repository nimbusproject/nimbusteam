<div class="box double-border">
<p class="centered-text bigger-text">Nimbus is cloud computing for science.</p>
</div>

<div class="box double-border">
<p><strong>Research.</strong> Cloud computing is a game-changing paradigm that raises challenging questions across many areas. Our team has been advancing the debate on how it should be best adapted to the needs of scientific projects through our research contributions.</p>
</div>

<div class="box double-border">
<p><strong>Development.</strong> Scalable, production-quality code is the best way to shape the cloud computing paradigm to the needs of science. Our team created the pioneering Nimbus toolkit and is now working on adapting cloud computing to the needs of Computer Science systems experimentation via CHI-in-a-Box.</p>
</div>

<div class="box double-border">
<p><strong>Operations.</strong> Science has unique needs that require unique clouds. Building and operating a cloud for computer science systems research requires support for high reconfigurability, advanced networking, and support for scientific rigor via repeatability. Visit us at <a href="https://chameleoncloud.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener">chameleoncloud.org</a> to see what it looks like!</p>
</div>
